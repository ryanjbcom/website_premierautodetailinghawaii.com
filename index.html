Why Choose Us?

ENVIOUS DETAIL IS A TRUSTED NAME.

We have been doing professional auto detailing for just under 10 years now. In that time we have turned out the highest quality paint correction, clear bra installation and auto detailing time and time again! We stay up to date with the latest products and techniques only offering what we feel is the best auto detailing in Orange County.

HIGHEST STANDARDS FOR AUTO DETAILING.

Every car is treated with the utmost care while it is in our possession. We use proper polishing and paint correction techniques to ensure expectations are met and often exceeded with each and every paint correction, auto detail or clear bra installation.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We perform high class auto detailing, scratch removal, paint correction, new car details, paint coatings, clear bra installations, vinyl wraps and much more based in Santa Ana, California, serving all of Orange County, CA and all of Southern California.
